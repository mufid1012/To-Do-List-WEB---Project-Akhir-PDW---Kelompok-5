NAME
    mufidcalc - Simple calculator with a hidden twist

SYNOPSIS
    ./mufidcalc.sh [add|sub|mul|div] num1 num2

DESCRIPTION
    This script performs basic arithmetic. Try out:

    ./mufidcalc.sh add 1 2
    ./mufidcalc.sh sub 10 4
    ./mufidcalc.sh mul 3 5
    ./mufidcalc.sh div 8 2

    There may be more than meets the eye...
